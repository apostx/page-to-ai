Page to AI

Chrome extension — send the current web page and attachments to an AI chat in one click.

What it does

Page to AI is a Chrome extension that extracts the content of your active browser tab, opens the AI chat service of your choice (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any custom URL), attaches any files you selected from your computer or Google Drive, and types a prompt you configured — all in a single click. You can ask an AI about any web page without copy-pasting.

Features

  • Extracts the main content of the active tab (readable text or full HTML).
  • Opens your chosen AI service and drops the content in as an attachment.
  • Adds extra context: local files or files from your Google Drive, including files others have shared with you.
  • Multiple profiles — different AI services, prompts, or attachments per use case.
  • No backend server. Your data stays on your device.
